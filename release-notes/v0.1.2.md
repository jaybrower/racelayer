# v0.1.2

_Released 2026-05-14_

## Added

- **Closing Rate column in Relative** — See how fast each driver is closing on or pulling away from you in **seconds-per-lap**, regressed over an 8-second rolling window of gap history.
  - **Sign is unified across rows** — positive always means closing
  - **Colour is player-perspective** — green when you're gaining ground (catching a car ahead, or pulling away from a car behind), red when you're losing it
  - **Calmer than naive Δ-gap math** — least-squares regression smooths the jitter you'd get from raw lap-distance precision, and a 0.05 s/lap noise floor stops the cells flickering when nobody's actually moving
  - Toggle per session-type in Settings → Relative
- **Pit-aware overlays** — overlays now adapt when the player is stopped instead of going haywire.
  - **Relative** detects when you're in the pit stall or approaching pits, pivots from gap-sorted to **position-sorted** slicing (keeping you as the visible anchor), renders `--` in the Gap column, and hides Closing Rate (both meaningless against pit-lane reference). A new **PIT badge** appears in the overlay header. Gap history clears on pit entry so the rate column doesn't show stale numbers when you rejoin.
  - **Pit Strategy — Tire Deg** filters pit-affected laps (out-laps, in-laps, and laps containing a full pit stop) from both the session-best baseline and the recent-laps wear window, fixing the previous behaviour where a slow out-lap could appear as +20s of "catastrophic tire deg" against your fastest flying lap.

## Changed

- **Brighter secondary text** across all overlays. Driver numbers, section titles, lap labels, and metric units are noticeably easier to read mid-race (`#475569` → `#94a3b8`).

## Fixed

- **In-app updater detecting v0.1.2 from v0.1.1** — the v0.1.2 release was initially missing `latest.yml` and the blockmap, and was flagged as a prerelease. Re-publishing with the manifest and removing the prerelease flag let `electron-updater` see the release. No code change, just a release-process fix; documented in CLAUDE.md so it doesn't recur.

## Install

- `RaceLayer-0.1.2.exe` — NSIS installer (recommended; supports in-app updates)
- `RaceLayer-portable-0.1.2.exe` — single-file portable build
- Already on 0.1.1? The in-app updater will detect and offer this release — Settings → Updates
